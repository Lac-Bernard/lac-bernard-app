---
title: Neighbourhood Watch Program
description: Information about the Neighbourhood Watch program.
---

The Neighbourhood Watch program reduces the opportunity for crime to occur through the active participation of citizens. Members are taught how to make their homes and property less inviting for thieves, how to participate in Operation Identification, and how to be alert to suspicious activity in their area.

Very few people are in a position to protect their homes and property all of the time and the Neighbourhood Watch program can help to provide a sense of security for individuals or families who leave their homes and cottages for any length of time. The program also helps to co-ordinate the efforts of the police and the community in tracking down criminals.
