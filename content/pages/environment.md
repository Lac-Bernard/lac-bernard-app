## Protecting Our Lake

The Association is committed to protecting the natural beauty and health of Lac Bernard for future generations. Our environmental programs include water testing, invasive species control, and shoreline protection initiatives.

## Our Programs

- **Water Sampling Program**: Regular testing to monitor water quality
- **Eurasian Milfoil Control**: Active management of invasive species
- **Shoreline Protection**: Initiatives to protect and restore natural shorelines
- **Water Protection**: Programs to maintain water quality
- **Boating Guidelines**: Safe and responsible boating practices
- **Wildlife Conservation**: Protecting the diverse wildlife that calls the lake home
