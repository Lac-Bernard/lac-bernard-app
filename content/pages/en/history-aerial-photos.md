Do you think you know the geography of our lake? **Would you be able to draw an outline of the lake simply from the impression you have in your mind?** Try it and then have a look at these aerial photos to see how close you came. Look a little closer, especially at the older photos, and you'll gain all sorts of insight into the changes that have taken place around our lake.
