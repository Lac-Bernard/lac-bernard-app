## Our History

Lac Bernard has a rich history dating back to the 1800s, with the original Fishing Club and subsequent Cottager's Association.

## Historical Timeline

- **1800s**: Original Fishing Club established
- **1930s**: Lac Bernard Cottager's Association formed
- **1979**: Current Association incorporated under Quebec Companies Act
