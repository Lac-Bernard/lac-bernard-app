title: Water Quality Results – 1986
description: Water quality sampling results for 1986.
---

Water quality testing was conducted in 1986 at more than 350 locations on Lac Bernard. The testing showed the water quality of Lac Bernard to be average, however we discourage residents from using the lake water for drinking.

The report and related documentation have been scanned (each page is a separate .jpg file) and can be downloaded as a zipped file: [1986WaterQuality.zip](/documents/1986WaterQuality.zip) (2,504 KB)

Or just the report, without related documentation, can be downloaded as a .pdf: [1986-SepticSystemStudy.pdf](/documents/1986-SepticSystemStudy.pdf) (5,150 KB)
