---
title: Properties and Deeds
description: Historical property records and deeds for Lac Bernard.
---

This section is intended to contain records that may help in conducting research on properties around the lake. Several documents have already been deposited but more need to be added. Land registries, surveys, real estate listings and individual property deeds are important sources that can offer much valuable information about land ownership, land use, property values, etc. throughout the history of the lake. If you have such documents, please get in touch with us… [archives@lacbernard.ca](mailto:archives@lacbernard.ca).
