## Our Mission

The Owners' and Residents' Association of Lac Bernard is a non-profit corporation created under Part 3 of the Quebec Companies Act. We work to build community, protect the lake, and promote safety.

## Our History

The Association was incorporated in 1979 as a successor to the Lac Bernard Cottager's Association, which began in the 1930s, with even earlier links to the original Fishing Club on the lake from the 1800s.

## Our Work

The Association prioritizes community building and environmental stewardship. We work across our community and with the municipalities of La Pêche and Low, as well as the MRC Des Collines-de-l'Outaouais, to further responsible development and environmental protection of our beautiful lake.
