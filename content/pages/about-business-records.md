---
title: Recent Business Records
description: Access recent Association business records and meeting minutes.
---

The folders in this section contain the most recent 2 years of Association business records such as the minutes of Board of Directors meetings, the Annual General Meeting and the Fall update to Members. The purpose is to enable members to have ready access to documents that will give them the most up-to-date information about the conduct of Association business.

Records generated in year 1 are retained until the end of calendar year 3, thus enabling all records, regardless of when they were generated in a given calendar year, to be retained for a full 2 years.

As soon as a record is made available in this section it is copied to the archives. As a result, even though it will eventually be deleted, it will be available in the Archives.

We hope this section will be useful in providing you with quick and ready access to the essential information you need in order to keep engaged in the work of the Association and to be an active participant in setting its strategic direction.
