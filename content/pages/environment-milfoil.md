## Eurasian Milfoil

Eurasian milfoil is an invasive aquatic plant that can significantly impact lake ecosystems.

## Current Status

**12% of the lake is now affected** by invasive milfoil. The Association actively monitors and manages milfoil populations to protect our waters.
