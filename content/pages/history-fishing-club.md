Did you know there was a very active organization on the lake called the Lake Bernard Fishing Club? Check out the first documents below. The 'log' kept by the club and the 'Brief History' offer a fascinating insight into the history of not just the club but also of the lake and its community through multiple decades from the 1890's to the 2000's. And as you will see in the rest of the records, the club was about much more than just fish!
