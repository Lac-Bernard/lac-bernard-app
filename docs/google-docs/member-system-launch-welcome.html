Member system launch: welcome email (draft copy)

Hi everyone,

We’re excited to let you know that the online member area on lacbernard.ca is ready. You can check your membership status, keep your contact details up to date, and this year you can pay securely by credit card right from your account. That’s something many of you have asked for.

Thank you for your patience while we put this in place.

What you need to sign in

Use the same email address the association already has on file as your main (primary) contact email. That way, when you sign in, the site can connect you to your existing member record, including your 2026 membership status and past years on file, if we have them.

If you use a different email than the one in our records, the site may not find your profile. Try another address with “Use a different email” on the sign-in page, or contact us and we’ll help align your email.

How to sign in

  • English: https://lacbernard.ca/en/membership/account/sign-in
  • French: https://lacbernard.ca/fr/membership/account/sign-in

You can Continue with Google or enter your email and choose Send magic link. We’ll email you a one-time link (no password to remember). (If you use Google, that Google email should be the same one we have as your primary contact.)

What you’ll see

After you sign in, you’ll land in your member area. You should see a short summary of your profile and your membership status for the current calendar year (the association follows the usual January–December year, based on Eastern time). When applicable, you’ll also see earlier years and anything prepaid for future years.

What you can do there

  • Update your profile: lake address, phones, mailing address, and optional secondary contact.
  • Start or renew your membership for the year when you’re not yet active.
  • Pay by credit card through a secure checkout, or use the other payment options shown on your account (e.g. Interac e-Transfer, cheque).

Paying by credit card

The big new piece for many of you is Pay with credit card. You’ll go through a short, secure checkout (handled by Stripe). You can add an optional donation to support the association in the same step if you wish. When payment succeeds, your membership should show as active on your account shortly after.

If you prefer not to use a card, everything you need for e-Transfer, cheque, or cash is still listed on the same page once you’ve started your membership request.

Quick steps: buy your 2026 membership online

  1. Open the sign-in link above (pick English or French).
  2. Sign in with Google or your magic link email.
  3. If the site asks you to create or complete your profile, fill it in and save, especially your lake address if you want General (voting) membership.
  4. On your account page, choose General or Associate, then click Start membership request.
  5. Click Pay with credit card and complete checkout, or follow the other payment instructions on screen.

That’s it. If you already have an active 2026 membership, you’ll see that instead of the payment flow.
